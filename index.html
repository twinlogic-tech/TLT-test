What we do

Twin Logic Tech was founded by two IT enthusiasts from an IT program in Helsingborg, Sweden, with a clear vision: to create a company that attracts talented collaborators and engages dynamic clients. While our current focus is website development, we aim to expand within the IT industry to offer tailored, comprehensive solutions. Whether for a large corporation or a small startup, our mission is to provide exceptional support, boost visibility, and drive innovation, helping your business thrive in the digital age.

Who we are

Peter Gustafsson

Co-founder & Developer

I'm Peter Gustafsson, a passionate tech enthusiast born in 1985 and a proud family man. After years in traditional roles, I pursued my passion for technology, focusing on front-end development and soon transitioning into full-stack. I've teamed up with a like-minded partner to launch a dynamic IT company offering services like website development, security, and tech installations.
